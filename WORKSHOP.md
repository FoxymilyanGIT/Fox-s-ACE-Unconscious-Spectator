# Fox's ACE Unconscious Spectator

A small client-side addon for ACE Medical.

This addon automatically opens ACE Spectator when a player becomes unconscious.

## Features

* Opens ACE Spectator when the player becomes unconscious.
* Allows spectating only living players from the same side.
* Keeps the unconscious player's body visible and treatable.
* Closes spectator when the player wakes up, dies or respawn.

## Requirements

* CBA_A3
* ACE3

## Notes

This addon does not replace ACE Medical or ACE Spectator.

The player's body is not hidden while spectating, so medics can still treat the player normally.
